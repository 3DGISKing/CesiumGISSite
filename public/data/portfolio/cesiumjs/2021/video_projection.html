Job description

We just monitored any modifications and solutions based on the CesiumJS library and found a cool feature with camera projection in the SuperMap project. And then my colleagues found your YouTube channel with different demos with images projections in Cesium and showed it to me.
So I decided to try to connect with you to find out if it is possible to make a feature like this for our project.
Our main goal is to implement outdoor cameras of our city with a projection of their signals onto a photogrammetry 3D model to make this part of the model alive with actual real-time video.
There are thousands of cameras in the city with streaming video, and each of them we want to project.

SuperMap ProjectionImage API
