Job description

The goal of this project is to calculate the 3d volume of terrain which is located above ground in a polygon area drawn by the user.

Solving story

Simple idea for this is to divide the terrain in the polygon area into numerous elementary cubic columns and sum the volume of the cubic column as shown in the figure below.
