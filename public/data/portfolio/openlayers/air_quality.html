Overview

This project was done by the request of NexGen Earth Labs(NGEL).
This map visualizes the time series of monthly mean PM2.5 mass concentration over 2000+ cities/monitoring locations worldwide.
The visualization provides quantitative changes for the past four decades using reanalysis data in absence of real measurements from ground monitoring stations.

Skills and expertise

OpenLayers, React, Material UI, Highcharts, PHP, PostGIS
